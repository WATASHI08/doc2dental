Dental Crown

Dental crowns are tooth-shaped caps that restore decayed or broken teeth. It also protects a weak tooth from breaking and to hold together parts of a cracked tooth.

Importance & Benefits:

Dental Crown supports the natural tooth with protection from being more weak and damaged, it preserves the natural tooth shape, structure, and function.

Benefits:

  • Restore the strength of natural tooth
  • Prevents tooth from undergoing root canal
  • Preserves parts of natural tooth
  • Restored shape and structure
